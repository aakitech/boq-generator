# Outreach Tracker

Tracking LinkedIn outreach to QS professionals and construction industry contacts for BOQ Generator.

**Goal:** Identify potential users, rate library contributors, and advisors. South Africa and Zambia focus.

**Status legend:**
- `pending` — message drafted, not sent
- `sent` — connection request or message sent
- `connected` — connected, no message yet
- `replied` — they responded
- `call-booked` — call scheduled
- `dead` — no response after follow-up
